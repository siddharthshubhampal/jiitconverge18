RULES & REGULATIONS
  • The competition is open to any college or semi-professional band from India.
  • After registering, send an email specifying the name and contact number of each band member to fortissimojiit@gmail.com
  • Each band would be allotted a time slot of 20 minutes (including amp set up and sound check).
  • Marks will be deducted if the time limit is exceeded.
  • Equipment provided will be: One lead amplifier speaker, microphones, drum set, acoustic guitar (you can bring along with your own cymbals and chokes/high hats).
  • Bands will have to bring their own musical instruments and special effects. However, programmed music is not allowed.
  • Winners will be announced shortly after the performances on the same day.
  • BANDS WILL BE DISQUALIFIED ON THE SPOT FOR MISCONDUCT, OBSCENITY OR FOUL LANGUAGE, AND WILL BE BANNED FROM PERFORMING AT ALL SUBSEQUENT EDITIONS OF CONVERGE FEST.
