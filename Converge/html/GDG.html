ABOUT THE EVENT
  • Dev-vogue is a Technical Quiz game of questions and answers on all topics of TECHNICAL knowledge that is played by technical students. Bring your grey cells to the party as Google Developers’ Group is back again this Converge’18 with its new fascinating event “DEV-VOGUE”.
    Far from classroom rote, this quiz is all about the Coding, Development, Web, Google, API’s etc. A competitive quiz contest with various rounds of different challenges. The questions and tasks will be completely based on development topics, basic coding and products, services offered by Google. .
ROUNDS DETAILS
  • 1. MCQ round:
       • It will consist MCQ questions.
       • Total time for this round will 20 mins.
       • Total 30 Question will be there.
       • 1 point will be awarded for each correct answer
       • Minimum 15 points are required to qualify for the next round.
  • 2. Dekho Likho Round
       • Participants will be shown problem through projector and participants need to solve the problem and note the answer.
       • Each question will be shown for at max 20 seconds.
       • 3 points will be awarded for each correct answer and 1 point will be taken for each incorrect answer.
  • 2. Surprise Round
       • Details of this round will be shared on the spot.
       • Only 6 teams can qualify for this round.
RULES & REGULATIONS
  • There is no registration fee.
  • Reporting Time: 10:40 am.
  • A team may consist of either 1 or 2 participant(s).
  • Round 1: Written Preliminary Round for all teams.
  • Round 2: Top 6 teams qualify for the Final Round.
  • Both rounds will take place on the same day. Time and Venue of the Final Round will be announced on the spot.

ABOUT THE EVENT
  • This simple format and time-tested team activity still makes one of the most popular team building events today. Teams armed with clues, questions and basic knowledge set off against the clock to be the first to complete challenges.
    We add the magic, creativity and theming to make your treasure hunt truly special and unique.

    Google Developers’ Group bring for you the something special this Converge’18. This time for hunting the treasure having Sherlock or Bond?, wont just help. Hunt for Google’s Treasure in a code based treasure hunt.
    Participants will play the event on an android app, where decoding question will take them to different locations in college. Ultimate aim is to find and aquire the Google’s Treasure.
GAMEPLAY
  • It will be a 2 Round Event.
  • First round will be played on an Android App, hence having an Android powered smartphone is must for participating team
  • Participants need to solve problems which lead them to a new a location inside college premises on reaching the location they need to scan a QR code which open there gate for a new problem.
  • First six teams solving all the problem will be qualifying for final round.
  • Second round is Surprise round, details of which will be told on the spot.
RULES & REGULATIONS
  • This is a team contest, however individual entries are also allowed.
  • The members have to be present onsite (Venue will be updated soon).
  • The contest will run for a time of 2 hours.
  • Teams can have at most three people.
  • There will be various tasks and each task will have points proportional to its complexity.
  • Team scoring maximum points will win.
  • Use of Internet is allowed.
  • Any use of unfair means will lead to immediate disqualification. Descion of coordinators will be final.
