Avengers: The Age of Ultron
EVENT DATE :
4th FEB 2017
EVENT TIME :
9:00 A.M.
EVENT VENUE :
Beside New Café.
INTRODUCTION
  • Set in an apocalyptic future, Ultron has returned to earth, and ruined it, using his superior technology to enslave the mankind. Certain villains do as Ultron commands and get higher privileges but at the end of the day Ultron rules. Most of the heroes live underground whilst thinking of a way to rid the world of Ultron. Amongst these heroes are Captain America, Hulk,Iron man and Thor.
  • The event follows the heroes plan to prevent Ultron from ever returning from the future, and the consequences of these actions.Here, Ultron has occupied a building. The avengers have to disinfect the cradles in the heart of Ultron to prevent him in accomplishing his vision of human extinction.
TASK
  • Teams must build a manually controlled bot which can do simple tasks of gripping blocks and complete the run with earning maximum points in minimum time.
  • The bot can be wired or wireless. In case the participants use a wireless mechanism, they must use dual frequency remote.
BOT SPECIFICATIONS
  • The dimensions of the bot should be less than or equal to 30 cm X 30 cm X 30 cm(length X breadth X height) at the start of the game failing which the team will be disqualified from the competition. However the bot can extend its dimension once the run starts.An error of (+5% /-5%) is permitted.
  • The bot must be controlled manually.
  • Teams can use both wired as well as wireless control mechanisms. In case of wired bots, the length of wire should be minimum 2 meters so that the wire remains slack at any instant of time. If the participants use wireless mechanism then it is mandatory to use a dual frequency remote.
  • The dimensions of the remote are not included in the size constraint of the bot. 5.The robots can use on-board or off-board will be provided.
  • Participants are not supposed to use any readymade lego components or readymade gripping mechanism. However the participants are allowed to use readymade gear assemblies. Violating this clause will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  • Irrespective of the mechanism used, only two members can enter the arena.
  • Failing to meet the above specifications will lead to immediate disqualification.
GAME-PLAY
  • There will be two arenas .In the first arena(gym area ) , the bot has to arrange the blocks of different avengers horizontally in order to cover the body of Ultron.The second arena is a multilevel track .The bot has to collect blocks(infected cradles)
  • from each avenger positioned at different floors by surpassing their respective hurdles and then place them on their respective places on Ultron's body.
SCORING
  • Scoring Points will be declared on the spot at the time of event.
RULES & REGULATIONS
  • The bot would be checked for safety before starting and will be disqualified if found unsafe for other participants.
  • Only one team member is allowed to handle the bot. No other team member is allowed to enter the arena.
  • The bot will be liable for disqualification if it causes any kind of damage to the arena or its components.
  • The bot is not allowed to slide the blocks against the ground except for fine adjustment .
  • Any damage done to the blocks will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • In case the bot gets stuck at any place, then the block it is carrying (if any) will be repositioned at its initial position and the bot will be kept in the checkpoints corresponding to that zone.
  • The blocks which are correctly deposited in deposit zones/pushed from ramp won’t be disturbed. The timer won’t be stopped during this process.
  • Two restarts are allowed in the round.
  • Team may consist of maximum of 4 members ,all with a valid identity card
  • Participants from different educational institutions can form a team.
ELIGIBILITY
  • All students with a valid identity card of their respective institutes are eligible to participate in the event.

Sherlock Holmes - Hounds of the Baskervilles
EVENT DATE :
4TH FEB 2017
EVENT TIME :
9:00 A.M.
EVENT VENUE :
CIC LAB
Introduction & Task
  • Your task is to build an autonomous bot trying to catch murderer in the Baskerville.
  • The bot needs to take correct decision at every point of the track that leads to the convict, in the shortest interval of time by traveling through the maze.
GAMEPLAY
  • The game play will be of single run
  • During this the bot has to start from the starting point and reach the end point and glow a LED light
  • Bot can take restart before reaching the 1st checkpoint on the track
  • The arena will be set up in ambient lighting conditions.
ELIGIBILITY
  • All students with a valid identity card of their respective institutes are eligible to participate in the event.
RULES FOR THE EVENT
  • Only one team at a time is allowed to run on the track
  • Two restarts are allowed in the round.
  • In case of a restart, the bot will be started from the starting point (origin).
  • No reorientation is permitted during the run.
  • The bot has to complete the task in minimum possible time (not more than 15 min).
BOT SPECIFICATIONS
  • Each team is allowed to have only one autonomous bot.
  • The bot must fit into a cuboid of 250mm x 250mm x 200mm. [Length x breadth x Height].
  • The bot must have self-contained locomotion and motor mechanisms and it should not communicate with any device using wireless data transfer.
  • The potential difference at any two points on the bot must not exceed 12 volts.
  • The teams can use readymade micro-controller circuits/sensor kits.
  • Teams can use readymade gear assemblies. However use of Lego kits is not allowed.
  • #Violation will lead to immediate disqualification from the competition.
POWER SUPPLY
  • The robots can use on-board or off-board power supplies.
  • The potential difference between any two points of the robots must not exceed 12 V DC.
GENERAL RULES
  • Each team can consist of a maximum of 4 members.
  • Participants from different educational institutions may form a team.
  • All students must bring a valid identity card of their respective institutions.
  • Hard coding will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Every team must get ready in specified time failing which will lead to disqualification.
  • The bot should cause no damage to the arena and surroundings by any means.
  • The bot has to be fully autonomous; human operators are not permitted to enter any information into the equipment during the run.
  • As the qualifying round, bots will undergo authenticity check, the participants should not dismantle their robots before the completion of the whole competition.
  • Organizer’s decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
SCORING
  • 5 points will be awarded for proper line following.
  • 20 points will be awarded if the bot completes the maze traversal.
  • 15 points will be awarded if bot traverses the levels correctly
  • 5 points will be awarded for correct detection of end of the maze.
  • 5 bonus points will be awarded for total solving of the maze.
  • # Winner will be decided according to maximum points earned by the bot.
  • # In case of tie-breaker winner will be decided by time taken to traverse the arena
  • *Scoring may be changed at the final event.

INTRODUCTION
  • In late 70’s there were two kings ‘Richard’ and ‘Harold’. Both of them love the daughter of king “Alfred the great” who was the king of state “Prytanis”.
  • Both ‘Richard’ and ‘Harold’ present their wish to marry “Isabella” but king ‘Alfred the great’ denied. So both of them got anger and announce the war with kingdom ‘Prytanis’.
  • But when both of them came to know that two of them has announced war on ‘Prytanis’ they place bet that the one who kill’s the king first will marry ‘Isabella’.

  • 3.     The bot will be liable for disqualification if it causes any kind of damage to the arena or its components.
  • 4.     Any damage done to the blocks will lead to immediate disqualification.
